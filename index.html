Dr John D. Ilee

Postdoctoral Research Fellow

School of Physics & Astronomy, University of Leeds

About

I’m an astrophysicist studying star and planet formation. I use state-of-the-art computer models to follow interconnected physical and chemical processes in protoplanetary discs. I utilise some of the most advanced telescopes in the world to observe these discs, determine their composition, and test models of their evolution.

I am the principle investigator of several programs studying discs around the most massive young stars using the Atacama Large Millimetre Array (ALMA).
